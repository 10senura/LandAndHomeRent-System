About Land & House Rental System

Our platform was created to simplify the property rental and buying process. We connect property owners with potential tenants and buyers, providing a streamlined experience for all parties involved.

With our user-friendly interface, comprehensive property listings, and secure booking system, finding your next home or investment property has never been easier.

Developed by Senura Indeewara at the Institute of Computing and Engineering Technology (ICET), our platform aims to transform the rental market with innovative technology solutions.
